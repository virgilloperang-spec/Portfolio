PGGP - Theme Migration

Project Overview

ProGuard Gutter Protection (PGGP) is a leading provider of high-quality gutter protection solutions. The company offers a range of products designed to keep gutters clean and free from debris, ensuring optimal performance and protection for homes and businesses. With a commitment to innovation and customer satisfaction, PGGP has established itself as a trusted name in the industry.


Objective

• Migrate the store from the old theme to a modern Shopify theme.
• Improve user experience and visual consistency.
• Maintain existing functionality during transition.
• Implemented a self-manageable storefront notification system that reduced    dependency on developers and improved content update efficiency.


Key Tasks

• Theme migration and configuration.
• Homepage redesign and layout improvements.
• Navigation and menu restructuring.
• Mobile responsiveness optimization.
• Form and lead-generation integration.
• Testing and quality assurance.

Shopify CLI Shopify Admin Liquid Canva
